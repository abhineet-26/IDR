# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Brand & Digital Presence

A comprehensive branding and web development project for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**, an industry-led institute dedicated to the training and deployment of digital, cyber, and AI risk practitioners.

## 🎨 Brand Identity

### Logo Design Philosophy
The IDR logo was designed to communicate **structure, resilience, and precision**.
* **Symbolism:** A geometric, cube-inspired icon represents the multi-dimensional nature of digital risk and the solid foundation provided by the institute.
* **Typography:** A clean, minimalist sans-serif font (Inter/Space Grotesk) was selected to bridge the gap between high-tech innovation and academic authority.
* **Palette:** * **International Orange (#FF4F00):** Symbolizes energy, alertness, and risk awareness.
    * **Deep Black & Stark White:** Provides the professional contrast required for regulated, high-consequence environments.

### Variants
* **Icon-Only:** Optimized for favicons, social media avatars, and mobile headers.
* **Icon + Text:** The full institutional signature for primary web and print application.

---

## 💻 Homepage Implementation

The IDR homepage is a custom-built, responsive single-page application focused on clear communication and a seamless user journey.

### Mandatory Sections Included:
1.  **Hero Section:** High-impact mission statement with a dual-focus call to action for programs and talent.
2.  **About IDR:** Detailed overview of the institute's role in regulated environments and its unique UK university partnership.
3.  **Service Pillars:** A structured card-based layout featuring the **Academy**, **Innovation & Incubation**, and **Advisory Services**.
4.  **Operational Model:** A visual representation of the *Train – Hire – Innovate – Deploy* pipeline.
5.  **Community Hub:** Segmented descriptions for students, early-career professionals, and industry practitioners.
6.  **Engagement Form:** A clean "Register Interest" interface for lead capture.

### Technical Requirements Met:
* **Pure Vanilla Stack:** Built exclusively with semantic **HTML5**, **CSS3 (Flexbox/Grid)**, and **Vanilla JavaScript**. No external frameworks (Bootstrap/Tailwind) were utilized to ensure maximum performance and clean code.
* **Responsive Architecture:** Fully fluid layout optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing.
* **Sticky Navigation:** Persistent header with smooth-scroll anchoring for intuitive section jumping.
* **Accessibility:** High-contrast ratios and legible web-safe typography compliant with modern web standards.

## ⚡ Setup & Preview

1.  **Locate Files:** Ensure `index.html`, `style.css`, and `script.js` are in the same root directory.
2.  **Launch:** Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3.  **Logo Assets:** PNG/SVG variants of the IDR branding are located in the `/assets/logo/` folder.
